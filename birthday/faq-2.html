Frequently asked questions

Sencillo will work at any location that uses Dropbox. Note that if Bluetooth is disabled on a company’s iPad, you’ll be unable to use Sencillo while the “Dropbox Sencillo is offline” notice is on the iPad screen.

Anyone with an iPhone or Android phone can use Sencillo.

If you have Dropbox at your office, you can use the Sencillo app to set your personal notification preferences. Also note: your Sencillo will not appear on your company’s iPad, but it will work when you visit other locations with Dropbox.

Dropbox iPads recognize Sencillo using Apple’s iBeacon technology. Since Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) is the underlying communication method for iBeacons, you’ll need to enable Bluetooth on your iPhone or Android phone.

Without Bluetooth, the Beacons will not “see” you, so you will not be able to use Sencillo.

This is a common misconception, but today’s Bluetooth technology, known as Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE), is different than the outdated Classic Bluetooth. BLE draws very little power and will have minimal impact on your phone’s battery life.
